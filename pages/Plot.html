ABOUT THE GAME

With compelling characters, an expansive world,
and an incredible amount of pirate activities to engage in,
Assassin’s Creed IV Black Flag is the largest and
most immersive Assassin’s game in the franchise.

GOLDEN AGE OF PIRATES

Assassin's Creed IV Black Flag begins in 1715,
when pirates established a lawless republic in the Caribbean and ruled the land and seas.
These outlaws paralyzed navies, halted international trade, and plundered vast fortunes.
They threatened the power structures that ruled Europe, inspired the imaginations of millions,
and left a legacy that still endures.
